Understanding SGPA, YGPA & CGPA

To better understand your academic performance, here’s how the calculations are done:

1. Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA)

Formula:
SGPA = (Σ Credit × Grade Point) ÷ (Σ Credits)

Example: If you have 3 subjects with credits [4, 3, 2] and grades [A=9, B+=8, A+=10], then:
SGPA = (4×9 + 3×8 + 2×10) ÷ (4+3+2) = 79 ÷ 9 = 8.78

2. Yearly Grade Point Average (YGPA)

Formula:
YGPA = (Σ Credit Points of Odd + Even Semester) ÷ (Σ Credits of Odd + Even)

3. Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA)

Formula:
CGPA = (Σ Credit Points of All Semesters) ÷ (Σ Total Credits of All Semesters)

4. CGPA to Percentage Conversion

Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 10
Example: If CGPA = 8.2, then Percentage = 8.2 × 10 = 82%
